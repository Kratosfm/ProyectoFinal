Vaccination schedule

  • At 45 days of age: first dose of the vaccine against Parvovirus.
  • At 9 weeks of age: the second vaccine that will be for protection against canine distemper, Adenovirus type 2, infectious hepatitis C and Leptospirosis. And the second dose of the vaccine against Parvovirus. The vaccine against Coronavirus is optional.
  • t 12 weeks of age: one dose of the previous vaccine and the third of Parvovirus are repeated.
  • From 4 months of age: the Rabies vaccine. Repeat annually the pentavalent vaccine (Parvovirus / Distemper / Hepatitis / Parainfluenza / Leptospirosis) and Rabies.
