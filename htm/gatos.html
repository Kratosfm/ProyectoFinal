Cats

Nutrition

It is very common to see that many people give their cat the leftovers of their food, or prepare a plate of special food. Sometimes, he is not aware of the risks of human food in cats. Their dietary needs are different from that of humans since their metabolism is different. It is 100% recommended that you feed your cats with special foods for them, in the current market there are many foods that are nutritious and taste good to feed them. Some foods that can be given to your cat as quirks are:

  • Cooked chicken or turkey (recommended for when they are sick).
  • Yogur (in very small quantities and not very often).
  • Eggs
  • Food for babies (check that there is no onion or garlic).
  • Bananas.
  • Butter and oil (small amounts in your food).
  • Rice.
  • Cooked vegetables.

How to educate a cat?

  • Do not punish him: To correct a bad behavior or to educate a cat begins by suppressing the scolding and punishments. Spend a little time per day playing or petting your pussycat; interact with him in the way you know he likes.
  • Give rewards: The other key point of how to educate a cat is to reward him every time he does something right.
  • Create unpleasant experiences: The third point of how to educate a cat is to make sure that inappropriate actions are unpleasant experiences.
